What We Collect

Photos, bio details, and location data to find matches.

How We Use It

To verify your identity and prevent fraudulent accounts.

Your Control

You can delete your data and account at any time.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, age, gender, and photos. We also use your device's GPS to show you matches in your area. We do not track your location when the app is closed unless you enable "Global Mode."

A Note on Verification

Our "Authentic Face" verification uses encrypted biometric markers to ensure your photo matches your identity. This data is used solely for verification and is never sold to third parties.

2. How We Share Information

Your profile is visible to other users within your discovery settings. We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third parties when required by law or to process payments for premium features.

3. Data Retention

We keep your information as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, your profile and messages are wiped from our active servers within 30 days.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@authenticface.com.
